Handover Form App

App Description
Internal Tool

A smoother transition when employees resign

Altair Handover is an internal tool designed to simplify the process of handing over client data when an employee resigns. The app ensures that no critical client information is lost and that the transition to the new employee runs smoothly.

How It Works

  1. Fill in client information through the provided form.
  2. Tap Next to proceed.
  3. A handover summary will be generated automatically.
  4. Tap “Copied to Clipboard” to copy the summary.
  5. Paste the summary into your internal group for seamless sharing.

Key Benefits

Structured, consistent handover of client data.
Quick copy-paste workflow for faster internal communication.
Prevents loss of important client details during offboarding.
Simple, intuitive interface for everyday use.

Status

Production Release — This version is stable and ready for use.

Privacy Policy

This application does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information entered is used only within your organization for handover purposes.
